ABOUT US

I, Aritra Bera, am a BCA student at MAKAUT University, Haringhata. My passion for both art and technology drives me to share my knowledge and expertise with young minds through personalized teaching. I offer one-on-one classes in both art and computer skills for students in grades 1 to 4, guiding each child on a journey of creativity and discovery.

My approach to teaching is unique, incorporating hands-on learning experiences with personalized attention and guidance. I focus on helping students develop their full potential, creating a supportive and engaging environment where they can thrive.

Whether your child is a budding artist or just looking to expand their computer skills, my classes are designed to bring out the best in every student. So why wait? Start your child's journey to success today and enroll them in my personalized art and computer classes.
